Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, sauté until translucent.
  3. Add ground beef, cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with Parmesan cheese.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes.
  8. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
